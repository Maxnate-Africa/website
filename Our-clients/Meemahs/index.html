About Us

Mary Marco Ndalahwa

Meemah’s Petal Palace is where flowers meet creativity. We are a Mwanza-based studio dedicated to designing moments that bloom. From luxury bouquets that speak volumes to personalized gift packaging and event setups that leave lasting impressions, our vision is to bring elegance and artistry to every occasion.

Founded by Dr. Mary Marco Ndalahwa, the Petal Palace is built on a passion for design and a commitment to quality. We believe every celebration, big or small, deserves a touch of beauty.

Our Services

Gift Wrapping & Packaging

Elevate your presents with our premium, creative gift styling. We use high-quality materials and artistic flair to make your gifts unforgettable before they're even opened.

Event Planning & Decoration

From engagements and birthdays to graduations and corporate functions, we handle the details. Our team provides full-service planning and decoration to create the perfect atmosphere.

Fresh & Artificial Bouquets

Designed to express emotion and elegance, our bouquets are artfully arranged using the finest fresh or high-quality artificial blooms. Perfect for any sentiment or celebration.

Funeral Wreaths

We create dignified and beautiful floral tributes to honor your loved ones. Our wreaths are thoughtfully designed to convey sympathy and respect during difficult times.

Car Decorations

Make a grand entrance or exit. We provide stylish floral decorations for weddings, new car celebrations, and other special processions, tailored to your theme and vehicle.
