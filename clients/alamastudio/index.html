Who We Are

A Studio Built on Passion

Alama Studios is a full-service creative agency based in the heart of Arusha, Tanzania. We're a passionate team of storytellers, designers, and technicians who believe in the power of a great image.

We do more than just capture moments; we build narratives. From dynamic videography and stunning photography to comprehensive branding strategies and high-quality printing services, we are your one-stop partner for bringing your creative vision to life.
